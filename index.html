CLOUD COMPUTING

Cloud computing provides on-demand access to computing resources over the internet, including servers, storage, databases, and software services. It enables businesses to scale resources dynamically, optimizing costs with pay-as-you-go models and fostering innovation through rapid technology deployment. Leading cloud providers like AWS, Azure, and GCP offer a range of services for infrastructure management, data analytics, machine learning, and more. Cloud deployments can be public, private, or hybrid, providing flexibility and customization options to meet diverse business needs.
